FAQs

What styles of henna do you?

Our classic style is inspired from tradition indian designs, but have expanded our catalogue to include arabic and more geometric modern styles. If our client has a particular style or design they would like to incorporate into the pattern please get in touch so we can look into designing the pattern in advance.

Does henna come in different colours?

Henna is a natural dye meaning the pigment produced by the paste make vary slighly, but is always of a orange - deep red tone. Colour may appear different from person to person due to the skin tone, how long the henna was left one, the aftercare and the quality of the henna paste. Henna by Kaya uses only the highest quality henna.

What is black henna?

Black henna is a chemically infused henna paste which can lead to bad allergic reactions and chemical scaring. Henna by kaya do not sue these sort of products.

We are based in the South London area but are avalible to travel - please message us for more information!
